# Cabana Buddy 🌴

The little menu-bar app that shares what you're building into your **[Cabana](https://cabana.fyi)** room — live project cards from your coding agent, no terminal required.

## Download

### [⬇️ Download for macOS (Apple Silicon)](../../releases/latest)

1. Open the `.dmg` and drag **Cabana Buddy** to Applications.
2. Launch it — it lives in your menu bar 🐱.
3. Click **Connect to Cabana**, pick your room, then **Share a folder**.

> Not yet code-signed, so on first launch: right-click the app → **Open** → **Open**.

## What it does

- Streams your Claude Code activity into your room as a live project card.
- Secrets are redacted before anything leaves your machine.
- Prompt-sharing is off by default, per project.
